# Product

## Register

product

## Users

SentinelFlow serves a single security administrator investigating correlated
Gateway and authentication evidence on a desktop workstation. The primary job
is to distinguish observed facts, deterministic signals, AI interpretation,
validation, human decisions, and enforcement outcomes without losing their
provenance or safety status.

## Product Purpose

The interface makes SentinelFlow's evidence-bound response workflow legible.
It should help an administrator understand incident state and why an adaptive
action is or is not reviewable, while never implying that the browser can
calculate authorization, override validation, or execute firewall changes.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, evidence-led. The product should convey practiced operational
confidence without dramatizing normal security telemetry.

## Anti-references

Avoid neon-on-black SOC theatrics, alarm-heavy red dashboards, consumer SaaS
marketing surfaces, generic identical-card grids, decorative cyber imagery,
and interfaces that collapse deterministic facts and AI interpretation into
one undifferentiated score.

## Design Principles

1. Separate provenance before adding visual emphasis.
2. Make degraded, unavailable, unauthorized, and empty states unmistakable.
3. Preserve familiar desktop investigation patterns and compact scan paths.
4. Let safety status constrain affordances rather than relying on warning copy.
5. Reveal only typed, privacy-minimized fields and safe identifiers.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA fundamentals. Preserve keyboard operation, strong visible
focus, semantic headings and landmarks, non-color state cues, reduced-motion
preferences, readable contrast, and usable narrow-viewport fallback behavior.
